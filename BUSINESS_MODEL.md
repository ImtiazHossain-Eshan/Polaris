## 1. Business Overview

### What Is Polaris?

Polaris is an **AI-native student admission strategist** that replaces fragmented Reddit advice and $2,000+ human consultants with a transparent, data-driven platform. It reverse-engineers competitive university profiles and builds personalized 6-to-18-month roadmaps for students — from middle school through graduate admissions.

### Business Model: SaaS + Marketplace Hybrid

Polaris operates on **two engines**:

| Engine | What It Does | How It Makes Money |
|--------|--------------|-------------------|
| **SaaS Core** | AI strategist, roadmap planning, probability engine, university discovery, deadline tracking | Monthly/yearly subscriptions (Free → Pro → Elite) |
| **Marketplace Layer** | Consultant bookings, partner offers, mentorship, community | Commissions, referral fees, listing fees, lead fees |

This dual-engine model means **revenue doesn't depend solely on subscriptions**. A Free user who never upgrades still generates marketplace revenue when they book a consultant or convert through a partner offer.

### B. Consultant Marketplace Commission

**Key principle: ANY user (including Free) can hire a consultant.** This is not gated behind Pro/Elite. Polaris earns a commission on every booking.

#### Commission Flow

```
Student pays $10 for visa consultation
    ├── Consultant receives: $8.00 (80%)
    └── Polaris keeps:       $2.00 (20%)
```

#### Commission Rate Analysis

| Rate | Polaris Take on $10 Booking | Consultant Gets | Viability | Notes |
|:---:|:---:|:---:|---|---|
| 10% | $1.00 | $9.00 | ⚠️ Too thin | Hard to cover payment processing + moderation |
| 15% | $1.50 | $8.50 | ✅ Viable | Competitive with Fiverr (20%), fair to consultants |
| **20%** | **$2.00** | **$8.00** | **✅ Recommended** | Industry standard (Upwork 20%, Fiverr 20%), sustainable |
| 25% | $2.50 | $7.50 | ⚠️ High | Risk of consultant churn; justify only with strong lead gen |

> [!TIP]
> **Recommendation: Start at 15% to attract initial consultants, scale to 20% once the marketplace has traction (100+ bookings/month).**

### F. Premium AI Usage Revenue

The current smart model router already routes between free (Gemini Flash), local (Ollama), and paid models. This creates natural upgrade paths:

| Tier | AI Access | Rate Limit |
|------|----------|:---:|
| Free | Gemini Flash, keyword fallback | 10 msg / 5 min |
| Pro | Gemini Flash, Groq (Llama-3.3) | 30 msg / 5 min |
| Elite | All models, premium routing | 60 msg / 5 min |

**Future AI monetization (without losing money):**

| Feature | Packaging | Cost Control |
|---------|----------|-------------|
| Deeper essay analysis | Elite only | Cache repeated patterns, limit to 3/month for Pro |
| Advanced probability sims | Elite only | Pre-computed results, on-demand only for Elite |
| Real-time "what-if" scenario runs | Elite only | Rate-limited, queued for non-peak |
| Multi-university comparison | Pro+ | Cache university data, only LLM call is the comparison |

**Cost management strategy:**
- Route simple queries (university info, deadlines) to **cached responses** — zero AI cost
- Route FAQ-like strategist questions to **Groq (free tier)** — zero cost
- Reserve **Gemini Flash** for roadmap generation and complex reasoning
- Use the **deterministic heuristic engine** as offline fallback — zero cost
- Monthly credit system: Free = 50 AI credits, Pro = 300, Elite = unlimited

## 6. Break-Even Analysis

## 11. Final Recommendation

### Best Business Model for Polaris

**SaaS-first, marketplace-second.** Subscriptions provide the predictable recurring base. The marketplace (consultants + partners) provides high-margin transactional revenue that monetizes the 90% of users who never subscribe.

### Best Monetization Order

| Phase | Timeline | Focus |
|:---:|---------|-------|
| **Phase 1** | Month 1–3 | Launch Pro/Elite subscriptions + consultant marketplace |
| **Phase 2** | Month 3–6 | Add partner referrals (AI-driven recommendations) |
| **Phase 3** | Month 6–9 | Introduce B2B partner listing fees |
| **Phase 4** | Month 9–12 | Community events, premium workshops |
| **Phase 5** | Year 2 | Premium AI credits, institutional partnerships, geo expansion |

### Which Revenue Stream to Launch First

> **Subscriptions + Consultant Marketplace together.** Subscriptions are straightforward. The consultant marketplace gives Free users a reason to stay and gives Polaris revenue from Day 1 without needing paid conversions.

### Which to Delay

> **Premium AI credits and institutional partnerships.** AI credit packs require usage data to price correctly (need 6+ months of data). University partnerships require brand credibility that takes time to build.

### Staying Ethical While Making Profit

| Principle | Implementation |
|-----------|---------------|
| Free resources first | AI always shows free options (Khan Academy, official IELTS prep) before paid partners |
| Honest labeling | Every partner offer shows its type: "Free benefit", "No commission", or "Commission — disclosed" |
| No fake locks | Free plan gives real value: full roadmap, benchmarks, deadlines, strategist |
| No demographic profiling | Probability engine uses transparent, non-demographic factors only |
| Consultant quality | Verification before marketplace listing; no unvetted editors |
| Coming-soon honesty | Unbuilt features show "Coming soon" tags, never as active benefits |
